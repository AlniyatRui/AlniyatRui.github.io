About

I am currently a Research Assistant in the CoSTA@NUS Lab, ECE, advised by Prof. Junyuan Hong. My research interests include large language models, machine learning, and robotics.

Before joining NUS, I received my master's degree in Computer Science from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), advised by Prof. Yanyong Zhang and Prof. Jianmin Ji. I received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Hefei University of Technology (HFUT), under the supervision of Prof. Kui Yu.
